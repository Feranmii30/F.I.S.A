About Me

Hello! I'm Feranmi Adeniyi (F.I.S.A), a passionate frontend developer and designer with over 3 years of experience creating digital experiences that users love. Based in Ibadan, Nigeria, I specialize in building responsive websites and intuitive user interfaces that solve real problems for businesses across Africa.

My journey into tech started at Appclick Academy, where I was first introduced to the world of web development. It was there I discovered a passion for building things that not only work but also feel good to use. Since then, I’ve worked with startups and clients, turning ideas into functional, user-friendly digital experiences. Every project has taught me something new and pushed me to grow, not just as a developer, but as a problem solver. I'm passionate about bridging the gap between design and functionality, and I’m always looking for new ways to create meaningful, impactful work.

When I’m not coding or designing, you’ll find me engaging with communities of developers.... sharing ideas, learning from others, and staying inspired. I believe in the power of collaboration and continuous growth, and I’m always eager to connect with others who are just as passionate about tech as I am.
